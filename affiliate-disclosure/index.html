Required by the FTC. Written plainly so you actually understand it.

Some links on this site earn me a commission. I only recommend products I'd actually use. Your price is never affected.

The short version

Sumimasen participates in affiliate programs. When you click certain links and make a purchase, I earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. This helps keep the site free and ad-light.

I was recommending these products before I had affiliate relationships with them. My editorial opinions are not influenced by commissions.

FTC disclosure (required by law)

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255), Sumimasen discloses that this site contains affiliate links. The operator of this site may receive compensation when you click on links and make purchases.

This disclosure applies to all pages on sumimasen. Affiliate links are not specially marked on every page, but this disclosure covers the entire site.
