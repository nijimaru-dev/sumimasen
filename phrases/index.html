Japanese Phrases That Actually Work

Tap to copy · Tap 🔊 to hear it · Tap ♥ to save for offline use

Calling the waiter Excuse me! (to get attention) すみません Sumimasen
Ordering from the menu I'll have this one, please. (while pointing) これをください Kore wo kudasai
Asking for the check Check, please. お会計をお願いします Okaikei wo onegaishimasu
Complimenting the food Delicious! (makes the chef happy) おいしい！ Oishii!
Asking directions Excuse me, where is [place]? すみません、[場所]はどこですか？ Sumimasen, [basho] wa doko desu ka?
Checking the train Does this train go to [destination]? この電車は[行き先]に行きますか？ Kono densha wa [ikisaki] ni ikimasu ka?
Buying a ticket How much to [destination]? [行き先]までいくらですか？ [Ikisaki] made ikura desu ka?
Asking the price How much is this? いくらですか？ Ikura desu ka?
Refusing a bag (eco-friendly) I don't need a bag, thanks. 袋はいりません Fukuro wa irimasen
Paying by card Can I pay by credit card? カードで払えますか？ Kaado de haraemasu ka?
Medical emergency Please call an ambulance. 救急車を呼んでください Kyuukyuusha wo yonde kudasai
Finding help Does anyone here speak English? 英語が話せる方はいますか？ Eigo ga hanaseru kata wa imasu ka?
Saying thank you (properly) Thank you very much. ありがとうございます Arigatou gozaimasu
When you don't understand I don't understand. わかりません Wakarimasen
Asking to repeat One more time, please. もう一度お願いします Mou ichido onegaishimasu
