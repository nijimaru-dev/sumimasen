Privacy Policy

Short version: we collect almost nothing. Here's the full version anyway.

This is a static website. We run no servers, store no accounts, and collect no personal data directly. The only data stored on your device is your saved phrases (localStorage), which never leaves your browser.

1. Who we are

Sumimasen is operated by Nijimaru (虹丸), an individual based in Japan. This site is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. For questions about this policy, contact us via the About page.

2. What data we collect

Data we collect directly: none

This site has no user accounts, no comment system, no contact forms, and no email sign-up. We do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information.

Data stored on your device (localStorage)

The Phrases page uses your browser's localStorage to save your favorite phrases. This data:

  • Is stored only on your device — never sent to any server
  • Contains only phrase IDs (e.g. "ph-r-01") — no personal information
  • Can be cleared at any time via your browser's "Clear site data" function
  • Is not used for tracking or analytics

3. Cookies

Sumimasen does not set any cookies directly. Third-party services embedded in this site (Google Fonts, Google Maps, affiliate links) may set their own cookies according to their privacy policies.

The Service Worker used for offline functionality stores cached versions of pages in your browser's cache storage. This is used only to make the site work offline and is not used for tracking.

4. Your rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete. For data held by third parties (Google, GitHub, affiliate partners), please refer to their privacy policies and exercise your rights directly with them.

If you are in the EU (GDPR) or California (CCPA/CPRA), you have rights regarding your personal data. Since we hold none, those rights are best exercised with the third-party services above.

5. Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through this site, please contact us.

6. Changes to this policy

We will update this policy if our data practices change (for example, if we add analytics or a newsletter). The "last updated" date below will reflect the most recent revision.

7. Contact

Privacy questions? Use the contact information on the About page.
